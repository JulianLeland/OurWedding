Accommodations & Travel

Our wedding will take place at Avery's parents' house, located at 1121 Springdale Rd. NE, in the Druid Hills neighborhood of Atlanta.

Accommodations in Atlanta

  • Emory Conference Center Hotel (~10 min. drive from wedding site): Special rates are available for guests of our wedding through this website. Guests may also call the hotel at 1-800-933-6679 to make reservations Mon- Fri 9am-5pm; ask for rooms in the Davis/Leland wedding block. We recommend booking as soon as possible to avoid missing the opportunity to book a room while there is still availability.

  • Emory Holiday Inn Express (~10 min. drive from wedding site)

  • Use AirBnB, VRBO or similar home-sharing services to find accommodations. We recommend the Druid Hills, Virginia Highlands, Candler Park, or Inman Park neighborhoods.

Travel To & Around Atlanta

  • Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport (ATL) is ~30 min. from the wedding and hotel sites.

  • Atlanta is a driving city! If you are flying in, we recommend using cabs, Uber or other ridesharing services, or renting a car.
